Day-1

Delhi - Mussoorie

Get ready to have a mesmerising experience in the land of utmost beauty, Uttrakhand.
Meet at a pickup point in Delhi.
Start an overnight journey from Delhi to Mussoorie.
Reach Mussoorie early in the morning on Day 2.

Day-2

Sightseeing in Mussoorie

On reaching Mussoorie, complete the check in formalities and leave for the Mussoorie local tour.
Visit famous Kempty waterfalls which is 15Kms approx. from Mussoorie.It is a popular tourist point in Mussoorie.
You can also visit Cloud Ends (at your own cost) which is 7Kms approx. from Mussoorie. It is the highest point of Mussoorie offering an awesome view to tourists.
Have lunch (at your own cost) and visit Camels Back Road which is around 3Kms where rocks are shaped like a hump of camel at the end of the road.
Later visit Gun Hill Point which is the second highest point in Mussoorie. You can also enjoy the view through a cable car at your own cost. It offers a complete view of Mussoorie.
In the evening visit Gun hill road and mall road for shopping.
Overnight stay and dinner at a hotel in Mussoorie.

Day-3

Mussoorie - Dehradun - Rishikesh

Wake up in the morning, have a good breakfast at the hotel and proceed to Dehradun after checking out.
On arrival at Dehradun, visit the most famous Sahastradhara waterfalls, Robberâ€™s Cave, Tapkeshwar Temple, Malsi Deer Park, Lachhiwala and Mindrolling monastery.
After exploring Dehradun, start driving towards Rishikesh which will take around 2 hours.
On arrival at Rishikesh, complete the check-in formalities, freshen up, take a tour of your hotel and head over to explore Rishikesh.
Enjoy the activities such as Bungee Jumping and River Rafting in Shivpuri (at your own cost). Shivpuri which is known for its river rafting activity is 16 KMS away from Lakshman Jhula.
In the evening, witness the Ganga aarti at Triveni Ghat.
Return to the hotel, freshen up and relax, enjoy an overnight stay and dinner at the hotel in Rishikesh.

Day-4

Rishikesh - Haridwar

Wake up early in the morning in Rishikesh, have a hearty breakfast and check out from the hotel.
Visit the famous bridges of Ram Jhula and Lakshman Jhula which span the breadth of the river Ganga.
After exploring the remaining places in Rishikesh, start your journey towards Haridwar.
Upon arrival in haridwar, complete the check in formalities and leave for Haridwar local tour.
Head over to see Daksha Mahadev Temple, Mansa Devi Temple, Vaishno Devi Temple and Dakshmahadev Temple.
Later in the evening, witness the famous Ganga Aarti and take a holy dip in Har ki Pauri.
Return to the hotel, enjoy an overnight stay and dinner at the hotel in Haridwar.

Day-5

Haridwar - Delhi

Wake up in Haridwar, have a hearty breakfast, complete the check out formalities from the Hotel then proceed to Delhi.
End the tour with lots of Golden memories on reaching Delhi.
